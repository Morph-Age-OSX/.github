## 🖌 Overview  
Morph Age Advanced is a professional image morphing application that creates smooth, fluid animated transitions between any two images. Whether morphing faces, animals, objects, or abstract shapes, it produces results with a level of quality that sets it apart from simple warping tools.

The curve-based morphing system uses splines rather than simple point grids, giving you precise control over how each region of the image transforms. The result is natural, organic motion that avoids the rigid artifacts common in lower-quality tools.

The preview engine renders the morph sequence in real time as you adjust control curves, letting you see and refine the animation interactively before committing to a final export.

Export options include QuickTime video, image sequences, and animated GIF, with full control over frame rate, duration, and resolution.

For digital artists, video producers, and creative professionals who need high-quality morphing output, Morph Age Advanced is the tool that delivers cinematic results with precise artistic control.
